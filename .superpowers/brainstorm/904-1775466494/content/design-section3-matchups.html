3b · Tournament Meta Advisor

3c · Blended Data Toggle

A toggle on the matrix lets you switch between three views: Untapped only (large sample, all levels of play), Tournament only (competitive top-cut data), and Blended (weighted average, default). Each cell shows the confidence level based on available sample size.
